President's Welcome


Welcome! My name is Elizabeth and I am the current President of Alpha Phi Omega at USC. On behalf of all our brothers, we would like to extend an invitation for you to join our brotherhood, where we dedicate ourselves to serving others. Through our cardinal principles of Leadership, Friendship, and Service, we aim to make an impact on both the local community and the lives of our members. I sincerely hope that you will join us as we make the world a better place!

In LFS,

Elizabeth Coughlan | President Fall 2018
